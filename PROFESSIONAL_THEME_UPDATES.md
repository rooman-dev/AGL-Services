# Professional Dark Navy Theme - Complete Updates

## Overview
Completely revamped the AGL Services website with a professional dark navy theme featuring excellent text visibility, proper color contrast, and modern design language.

---

## Color Palette Updates

### New Professional Colors
- **Primary Blue**: #1e3a8a (Rich Navy)
- **Dark Background**: #0f172a (Deep Navy)
- **Secondary Background**: #1e293b (Slate Navy)
- **Accent Blue**: #3b82f6 (Bright Blue)
- **Text Color**: #f3f4f6 (Off-white for perfect contrast)
- **Shadow**: Deep shadows with 0.8+ opacity for depth

## Key Improvements Made

### 1. **Text Visibility & Contrast** ✅
- Changed text color to #f3f4f6 (off-white) for maximum contrast against dark backgrounds
- Increased font weights (500-700) throughout for better readability
- Added text shadows on headings for depth
- All text now WCAG AA compliant

### 2. **Component Styling** ✅

#### Navigation Bar
- Gradient background: #0f172a to #1e3a8a
- Blue accent border bottom (#3b82f6)
- Improved shadow and spacing
- White text with better contrast

#### Service Cards
- Semi-transparent backgrounds (rgba(30, 58, 138, 0.4))
- Blue accent borders with proper transparency
- Better shadow effects
- Professional icon gradients

#### Contact Cards
- Clean borders with blue accents
- Icon gradients from #3b82f6 to #1e3a8a
- Improved hover effects with scale and color transitions
- Better text contrast

#### Buttons
- **Primary Button**: Bright blue gradient (#3b82f6 to #1e40af)
- **Secondary Button**: Semi-transparent blue with blue border
- Enhanced shadow effects
- Better hover states

### 3. **Background Gradients** ✅
- Replaced light cyan gradients with professional dark navy
- Added subtle accent gradients with blue tones
- Maintained animated background movement
- Improved visual hierarchy

### 4. **Font Weights & Typography** ✅
- Body text: font-weight 500 (medium)
- Headings: font-weight 700-900
- Form labels: font-weight 600-800
- Links: font-weight 700
- Enhanced line-height for readability (1.6-1.8)

### 5. **Form Elements** ✅
- Dark backgrounds with blue borders
- Better placeholder contrast
- Focus states with blue highlight
- Proper label visibility

### 6. **Footer** ✅
- Dark navy background with blue gradient
- Blue top border for visual separation

- Better social icon styling
